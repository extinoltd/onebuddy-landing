Frequently Asked Questions

Is OneBuddy as effective at blocking ads as uBlock Origin?
OneBuddy covers the most common ad types: YouTube ads, popups, banners, and overlays. uBlock Origin supports more granular control via custom filter lists, which power users may prefer.
Can I use both together?
Technically yes, but running two ad blockers may cause conflicts. OneBuddy is designed to replace uBlock Origin entirely while adding productivity and privacy tools.
Which is lighter on resources?
Both are efficient. OneBuddy uses slightly more resources because it includes many more features, but the difference is negligible for modern hardware.
