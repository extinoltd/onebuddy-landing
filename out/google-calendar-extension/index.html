Frequently Asked Questions

How does the Google Calendar integration work?
OneBuddy uses Google's official OAuth 2.0 flow to securely connect to your Google Calendar. Your password is never stored — only a secure access token.
Will I get notifications for upcoming events?
Yes. OneBuddy uses Chrome alarms to send notifications before your events, so you never miss a meeting.
Can I create or edit events from OneBuddy?
Currently, OneBuddy provides a read-only view of your upcoming events with notifications. Event creation and editing happen in Google Calendar.
Is the calendar sync real-time?
OneBuddy periodically syncs with Google Calendar to keep your event list current.
