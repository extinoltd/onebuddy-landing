Frequently Asked Questions

How do slash shortcuts work?
Define a shortcut like /greet and map it to a full text template. When you type /greet in any text field, it auto-expands into your template.
Does it work in Google Docs and Gmail?
Yes. Slash shortcuts work in any web-based text field including Gmail, Google Docs, Slack, and more.
Is there a limit to how many shortcuts I can create?
You can create as many shortcuts and notes as needed within Chrome's local storage limits.
Can I export my notes?
Notes are stored locally in Chrome's storage. You can copy note content to your clipboard for export.
